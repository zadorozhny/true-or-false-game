Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-27

True or False: Flags Game is designed to respect your privacy. We collect only the information needed to operate the app, support leaderboards, improve stability, and show ads.

Information we collect

  • Anonymous gameplay data (scores, regions played, mistakes, time taken)
  • Device and diagnostic data (app version, OS, crash reports, performance data)
  • Ad-related data (ad impressions, ad interactions, and basic device information used to serve and measure ads)

We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise GPS location.

Advertising

The app may show ads, including rewarded ads (optional ads you can watch to earn extra games or similar in-app benefits). Ads are provided by third-party partners (for example, Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads).

These partners may collect information such as your IP address, device identifiers, coarse location (derived from IP), ad interaction data, and diagnostic data to serve, limit frequency, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

On iOS, access to the device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) depends on your system settings and, where required, your permission. If you don’t allow tracking, ads may still be shown but may be less relevant.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the game
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Maintain leaderboards and best results
  • Show ads and measure their performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Third-party services

The app relies on platform services (such as Apple) and third-party providers (such as ad networks and backend infrastructure) to operate. These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Ads may still be shown, and ad partners may process limited device information to serve and measure ads.

Your rights

Depending on your location (GDPR / CCPA), you may request access or deletion of stored data. Since the app does not collect account-based personal identifiers, requests may be limited. You can also control certain ad permissions through your device settings.
