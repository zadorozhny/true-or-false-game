Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-19

True or False: Flags Game is designed to respect your privacy. We collect only minimal information required for the app to function.

Information we collect

  • Anonymous gameplay data (scores, regions played, time taken)

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the game
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Maintain leaderboards and best results

Third-party services

The app may rely on platform services (such as Apple) and backend infrastructure to operate. These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

Depending on your location (GDPR / CCPA), you may request access or deletion of stored data. Since no personal data is collected, most requests require no action.
